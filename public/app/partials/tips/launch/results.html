"Launch results"

On this page you can see launch results and relaunch tests.

Counter

Show passed, failed, skipped, blocked test results and other information.

To create connection with jira issue fill two fields:

Issue Id - existent jira issue key
Regex - regular expression

Tasks

Show progress of your launch items.

Relaunch

Launch tests with the same environment variables.

Terminate

Terminate button is available only for launch in progress.
